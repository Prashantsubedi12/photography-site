Where We Shoot

All sessions take place at iconic Osaka locations — chosen based on your style and the vibe you want. Here are my 10 favourite spots.

01

Dotonbori

道頓堀

Osaka's neon soul — canal reflections, the Glico sign, and electric street energy. Best at blue hour, 6–7 PM.

02

Osaka Castle

大阪城

Classic Japan — historic castle against the modern skyline. Stunning in every season, magical during cherry blossom season.

03

Nakazakicho

中崎町

Osaka's most photogenic retro neighbourhood. Narrow lanes, hand-painted signs, wooden facades, and almost zero crowds on weekday mornings.

04

Umeda Sky Building

梅田スカイビル

Twin towers connected at the top with a glass escalator. Futuristic architecture and panoramic city views — especially powerful at sunset.

05

Shinsekai

新世界

Osaka's retro-neon district. Tsutenkaku Tower, colourful signage, narrow streets. Best late afternoon into evening for warm neon tones.

06

Nakanoshima

中之島

3 km of riverside parks and grand Meiji-era Western architecture. Rose gardens, iron bridges as frames, and golden-hour glow on the water.

07

Hozenji Yokocho

法善寺横丁

A moss-covered stone lantern alley just off Dotonbori. Quiet, cinematic, and intimate — perfect for portraits that feel completely different from the neon chaos.

08

Kuromon Market

黒門市場

A covered arcade full of colour, steam, and texture. Consistent shade means great light any time of day. Unique backdrop for lifestyle and candid portraits.

09

Sumiyoshi Taisha

住吉大社

One of Japan's oldest shrines — the iconic red arched bridge and traditional architecture. Quiet, beautiful, and deeply Japanese.

10

Tennoji

天王寺

Tennoji Park's greenspace and the urban energy of the surrounding city. Great contrast between nature and city, with long street perspectives.

About Me

Hello, I'm Prashant

21 years old, based in Osaka. I'm a bilingual photographer (English & Japanese) with a passion for capturing authentic human moments. What started as a way out of a dark place became the thing I love most — and now I want to share that with you.

My Story
My Philosophy

Photography is about feeling, not just seeing.

Before every session I talk to you. Not about poses, not about lighting — about you. I want to understand how you see yourself, how you'd like to be remembered, and what makes you feel at ease. Only then do I pick up the camera.

I don't create shots — I recognise them. The best moments happen between the planned ones. My job is to be present enough to catch them.
